About

I sit at the intersection of finance and virtual assistance — a combination that's rarer than it should be. Most VAs manage your calendar and inbox. I do that too, but I can also reconcile your bank statements, process your invoices, and turn your operational data into a report you'd actually trust.

That comes from real experience: as an Invoice Clerk Intern at Mogo Auto Limited, I process high-volume transactions inside an ERP system, run bank reconciliations, manage accounts receivable, and produce management reports — daily, not hypothetically.

I'm currently deepening the VA side through ALX-Africa's Virtual Assistance certification, and the analytics side through Google's Data Analytics Certificate, so I can support clients who need their numbers not just accurate, but understandable.
